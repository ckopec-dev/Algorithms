# Borůvka's Algorithm in COBOL

Here's an implementation of Borůvka's algorithm for finding Minimum Spanning Tree (MST) in COBOL:

```cobol
       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. BORUVKA-MST.
       AUTHOR. COBOL IMPLEMENTATION.

       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       INPUT-OUTPUT SECTION.
       FILE-CONTROL.
           SELECT MST-INPUT ASSIGN TO "GRAPH.DAT"
               ORGANIZATION IS LINE SEQUENTIAL.

       DATA DIVISION.
       FILE SECTION.
       FD  MST-INPUT.
       01  INPUT-RECORD.
           05  NODE-A          PIC 999.
           05  NODE-B          PIC 999.
           05  EDGE-WEIGHT     PIC 999.

       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01  GRAPH-STRUCTURE.
           05  NODES           PIC 999 VALUE 0.
           05  EDGES           PIC 999 VALUE 0.
           05  MAX-NODES       PIC 999 VALUE 100.
           05  EDGE-TABLE.
               10  EDGE-RECORD OCCURS 1000 TIMES.
                   15  EDGE-START    PIC 999.
                   15  EDGE-END      PIC 999.
                   15  EDGE-WT       PIC 999.
           05  NODE-SET.
               10  NODE-SET-RECORD OCCURS 100 TIMES.
                   15  NODE-ID       PIC 999.
                   15  NODE-ROOT     PIC 999.
                   15  NODE-LEVEL    PIC 999.
           05  MST-RESULT.
               10  MST-EDGE OCCURS 100 TIMES.
                   15  MST-NODE-A    PIC 999.
                   15  MST-NODE-B    PIC 999.
                   15  MST-WEIGHT    PIC 999.
           05  TEMP-VARIABLES.
               10  TEMP-NODE     PIC 999.
               10  TEMP-WT       PIC 999.
               10  TEMP-ROOT     PIC 999.
               10  TEMP-LEVEL    PIC 999.
               10  TEMP-FOUND    PIC X VALUE 'N'.
               10  TEMP-INDEX    PIC 999.
               10  TEMP-FOUND-INDEX PIC 999.
           05  CONTROL-VARIABLES.
               10  CURRENT-NODES PIC 999.
               10  MST-EDGES     PIC 999.
               10  ITERATION     PIC 999.
               10  MAX-ITERATIONS PIC 999 VALUE 100.
               10  FOUND-MST     PIC X VALUE 'N'.
               10  DEBUG-FLAG    PIC X VALUE 'N'.

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       MAIN-PROGRAM.
           DISPLAY "Borůvka's Algorithm for MST"
           DISPLAY "=========================="

           PERFORM INITIALIZE-GRAPH
           PERFORM READ-INPUT-DATA
           PERFORM BORUVKA-ALGORITHM
           PERFORM DISPLAY-RESULTS
           STOP RUN.

       INITIALIZE-GRAPH.
           MOVE 0 TO NODES, EDGES, MST-EDGES
           MOVE 0 TO CURRENT-NODES
           MOVE 0 TO ITERATION
           MOVE 'N' TO FOUND-MST

           PERFORM INITIALIZE-NODE-SETS
           PERFORM INITIALIZE-EDGE-TABLE.

       INITIALIZE-NODE-SETS.
           PERFORM VARYING TEMP-INDEX FROM 1 BY 1
               UNTIL TEMP-INDEX > NODES
               MOVE TEMP-INDEX TO NODE-SET-RECORD(TEMP-INDEX)
               MOVE TEMP-INDEX TO NODE-ROOT(TEMP-INDEX)
               MOVE 0 TO NODE-LEVEL(TEMP-INDEX)
           END-PERFORM.

       INITIALIZE-EDGE-TABLE.
           PERFORM VARYING TEMP-INDEX FROM 1 BY 1
               UNTIL TEMP-INDEX > EDGES
               MOVE 0 TO EDGE-RECORD(TEMP-INDEX)
           END-PERFORM.

       READ-INPUT-DATA.
           OPEN INPUT MST-INPUT
           READ MST-INPUT AT END GO TO READ-INPUT-END
           PERFORM PROCESS-INPUT-RECORD
           READ MST-INPUT AT END GO TO READ-INPUT-END
